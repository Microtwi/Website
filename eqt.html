About & Updates

About:

Genre: An Fantasy Action RPG Game
Description: Equinox Tales is an action-adventure, role-playing game set in a fantasy world (The Equinoxia Multiverse / Galaxy), for ages 16+.

Embark on a journey through a world full of mysteries, challenges. In this narrative-driven adventure, you play as Rusty, Nova or Stella, some, the main characters of this world.
Play as unique characters with their own strengths, weaknesses, and ways to handle challenges. Face battles, trade smart, and grow your confidence in a world that reacts to how you play.

The first chapter will introduce you to the main character, Stella, but will also put her in front of many dangers, that she needs to overcome.

Here are some short descriptions of some characters, Nomand and Stella:

Stella

She is the queen of the medieval village Meadowvale, a skilled warrior haunted by her past. She fights to overcome her fears but often struggles when things go wrong. Her resolve grows with training and deep thought.
Nomand

A merchant at heart, always looking for opportunities. He fights not for glory but to secure his future wealth.

Gameplay & Screenshots

Confidence System

Your character preferred activities, fears, but also the small victories changes their confidence. Adapt your strategy to each character trait.

Because of how one mind affects their performance, the confidence bar also serves as your reputation

You get reputation points for defeating enemies!

Combat

Engage in an fast paced combat system, where you take on multiple enemies at once, however be careful of your confidence meter as it affects the combat in different ways.

Tip: Don't let it hit 0%.

Play as any character you want!

Prefer to play as Stella over Nova? No problem, you can switch the character without impacting gameplay!

Immersive Storytelling

Uncover the secrets of the world, delve into the emotional depth of your characters, and make choices that will shape the characters.
