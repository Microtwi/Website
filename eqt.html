About & Updates

About:

Genre: An Fantasy Action-Adventure RPG - Age Rating: 16+
Description: Equinox Tales is an action-adventure RPG set in the Equinoxia Multiverse / Galaxy.

Embark on a journey through a world full of mysteries, challenges, some light horror and talking equines. Play as Cedric, Rusty, Nova, or Stella; each with unique strengths and fears which will impact how you handle battles.

At the core of the game is the Confidence System, which reflects a character's mentality, how confident or scared they are. Confidence directly impacts combat performance, and each character has unique ways of gaining or losing it. You'll need to adapt to their personalities and fighting styles to succeed.

The first chapter follows Stella, the queen of Meadowvale, who must push trough dangers despite a her tragic past; setting the stage for this emotional roller coaster, narrative-driven story.

Here are some short descriptions of some characters you meet:

Stella

A skilled warrior haunted by her past. She recovers confidence through small victories and every landed strike, but each loss or hit cuts deep.
Cedric

A stubborn fighter who confidence thrives on big wins, risk-taking, but struggles with small setbacks.
